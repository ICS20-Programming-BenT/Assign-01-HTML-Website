For as long as I can remember, I've always wanted to travel. Places like the UK, Barbados, Peru and Scotland come to mind when I think of a place to go. But one place stands out; Greece. With the beautiful beaches, lots of stories and exceptional culture and food, Greece is where I want to go the most.

What I would like to do in Greece:

Here are a few places I would like to visit, along with some activities I would like to do.

  1. Athens is the capital of Greece and it is a very important place in a historical context. This is the city that hosted the first Olympic Games! I would like to go to the Acropolis of Athens to see the Parthenon and the Erechtheion. I also want to visit the National Museum of Archeology in Athens, because it is a very popular place to learn about the incredible archeology of Greece.

  2. Santorini is the classic Greek postcard. If you like views from high cliffs overlooking the ocean, Santorini is the place to be. I want to visit Ammoudi beach because of the turquoise water and white sand. I would also like to watch the beautiful sunset overlooking the cliffs.
