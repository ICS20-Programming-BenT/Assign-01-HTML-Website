Here are some images of France:




France is one of the countries I am most excited to visit. Not only will I get to practice my French, but I will also be able to visit some of the most famous attractions in the world, such as the Eiffel Tower, the Musée du Louvre, the Château de Versailles, Mont Saint-Michel and the Côte d'Azur.

Here is a list of some interesting facts about France:

  • France is the most popular tourist destination in the world, 89.3 million people visited the country in 2018.
  • The highest mountain in Europe is located in France, called Mont Blanc.
  • The Louvre Art Museum in Paris is the most visited museum in the world, with works of art such as the Mona Lisa featurd inside.
  • Turning a baguette upside down is considered a sign of bad luck in France.
